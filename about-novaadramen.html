Novaadramen

What is Novaadramen?

Novaadramen is an independent HR-tech and systems design initiative founded by Rajiva Mendis. It focuses on building working prototypes and proof-of-concept systems for HR automation, analytics-driven workflows, and AI-assisted decision support—especially for hospitality and service operations.

Who is Rajiva Mendis?

Rajiva Mendis is a Human Resources professional and self-taught systems builder based in the UK. His work blends practical HR operations knowledge with Python, data analytics, and structured system design to create tools that make workplace processes clearer, fairer, and measurable.

What does Novaadramen build?

  • SCRIBTUM — Local-first knowledge and reasoning system (Python/SQLite) powering long-term thinking, audits, and debugging across projects.
  • SultanGate — Working HR induction system prototype for hospitality onboarding, video-led inductions, and quiz tracking.
  • ExitGate — Exit interview and turnover analytics proof-of-concept to capture confidential feedback and retention insights.
  • PAeryllMate — Payroll/rota calculation proof-of-concept designed for shift-based pay forecasting and reconciliation.
  • PoliSense AI — Conceptual AI-assisted HR policy reasoning system (design phase).
