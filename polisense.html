What is PoliSense AI?

PoliSense is a conceptual AI-assisted HR policy reasoning system designed to support managers in interpreting HR policies, labour laws, and compliance frameworks. It is intended to guide users through legally sound, culturally aligned decision-making scenarios.

The concept explores how AI-driven reasoning can assist HR teams in identifying workplace risks, escalation paths, and appropriate responses before issues intensify.
