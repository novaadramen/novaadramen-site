ExitGate HR

Virtual Exit Interview & Turnover Analytics for Hospitality HR

How the Idea Was Born

During my time managing HR operations in the hospitality industry, I noticed a recurring gap in how staff exits were handled. Valuable feedback was often lost, exit interviews were inconsistent, and turnover insights were buried in paperwork. ExitGate HR was born out of a vision to transform these moments of departure into measurable insights.

System Introduction

ExitGate HR is a digital prototype built for The Celestial Sultanate Hotel*. It offers departing staff a secure, confidential platform to complete exit interviews, while delivering real-time turnover dashboards to HR management.

*The Celestial Sultanate Hotel is a fictional property created for project demonstration purposes only.
